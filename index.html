Bob Managerman

Bob Managerman is a highly respected Executive Branch Manager with over 7 years of experience—and absolutely zero suspicion from his coworkers that he is, in fact, a cat in a very convincing business suit. With a sleek résumé and an even sleeker coat of fur hidden beneath his tailored three-piece ensemble, Bob has risen through the ranks with a purrfect blend of charm, cunning, and inexplicable charisma.

Bob excels at strategic planning, team leadership, and knocking pens off desks when no one is looking. His inbox is always clear, his whiskers always twitching at the scent of inefficiency. Known for his uncanny ability to nap with his eyes open during meetings, his ruthless efficiency in chasing down quarterly goals (and laser pointers), and his unsettling knack for showing up silently behind people, Bob has climbed the corporate ladder with the quiet grace of a predator stalking a rogue spreadsheet.

Outside of meetings, Bob can often be found basking in sunbeams on the office windowsill, hiding inside filing cabinets, or staring unblinking at interns until they rethink their entire presentation strategy. His performance reviews are flawless, though his signature remains suspiciously shaped like a pawprint. He’s also notorious for mysteriously vanishing whenever someone mentions “mandatory team-building exercises.”

When not herding humans, pushing mugs off the break room counter, or approving budgets with a single decisive tap of the paw, Bob enjoys cardboard boxes, judging others silently from high places, and pretending not to understand how Zoom backgrounds work.

A true professional. A dedicated leader. Definitely not hiding a tail under that pinstriped suit. Probably.
